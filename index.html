Fractal Jigsaw Puzzle

In this jigsaw puzzle, the pieces are themselves puzzles made of smaller pieces. Solve the puzzles from the smallest to the largest and end up with a nice image from the Mandelbrot set.

  • This program runs in your web browser.
  • Blank pink pieces need to be solved. Double-click on them and get to work.
  • Red pieces are already solved and will be filled in by the computer. This process takes a few seconds per piece.
  • Drag pieces with your mouse. If a solved piece is moved close to its correct position, it will click into place.
  • You can form free-floating groups of solved pieces.
  • Press Escape if you double-click on a solved piece.
  • Press S to toggle sound on and off.
  • Your progress is saved.
  • There are a lot of pieces in this puzzle. I don't expect anyone to finish it.
